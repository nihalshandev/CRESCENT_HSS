CRESCENT HSS
VANIMAL

Vanimal Crescent High School was founded in 1976 in the heart of Vanimal Panchayat in the eastern hilly district of Kozhikode district. The school has played a vital role in educating this area where education was backwards. Students of this school have been able to achieve different positions at school level and in school competitions. The Vavuniya Crescent High School, which operates only 8,910 classes, is a vocational school as well as in the curriculum as in the field of study, has raised this school as a higher secondary school in 2014
